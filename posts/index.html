Dry aging process

Provide Feedback temperature: 32-39.2deg If the temperature is above this, the germs multiply. At temperatures below the freezing point, the enzymes can not work and the meat does not ripen. humidity: 80-85% It is possible to vary the humidity easily between 60-90%. This slightly changes the flavor, which makes the final product more individual. At higher values, however, the possibility of mold attack increases. At low levels, the outer crust dries deeper and deeper.
